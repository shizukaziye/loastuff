Every class enters at the stronger of its two engravings. The number on each card is its players’ average rDPS divided by their Combat Power, so gear cancels out and only the kit remains. Tiers are fixed bands of #1’s value: S ≥98% · A ≥95% · B ≥90% · C ≥85% · D ≥80% · F below.

Method & fine print

  • Metric. “rDPS by Combat Power” from lostark.bible raid statistics (self-reported LOA Logs data): average rDPS ÷ Combat Power per logged player. Data refreshes on demand through a Cloudflare worker cache; the worker re-pulls anything older than 24 hours when you look it up.
  • One class, one row. We rank each class by its strongest engraving; hover a card for the other engraving’s value.
  • Exclusions. We leave out Bard and Artist, along with support specs (Blessed Aura, Desperate Salvation, Full Bloom, Liberator, “Princess” Gunlancer) — support rDPS is buff attribution on a different scale.
  • Tiers. Fixed percentage bands of the #1 class’s value — the dashed lines on the spread. A tier can be empty; that is information, not an error.
  • † Small sample. Entries with n < 300 rest on few logs — read those placements loosely.
  • Retired raids (Behemoth, Aegir, Brelshaza, Mordum, Extreme Thaemine, Tarkal) stopped accepting stats before Combat-Power data collection began — no chart exists for them.
  • Icons from the astrogem calculator. Site source: github.com/shizukaziye/loa-tierlist. Not affiliated with Smilegate or lostark.bible.
